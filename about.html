About

My Story

I grew up in western Massachusettes, spent a lot of time in the woods and realized a passion for music and performance. Working in the DIY / Underground music community as a touring musician and independent label head gives me a unique creative perspective that supports fresh solutions. Through collaboration and recording, I have learned temperance and flexibility that vastly benefit my designs. During the pause to live music caused by the Covid 19 pandemic I chose to dedicate my time to learning relevant design skills with a goal of enhancing my value to my community and demystifying the digital experience for others.
