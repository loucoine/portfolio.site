My Story

I grew up in the woods of Massachusetts, formed a band out of highschool and followed music around the world. I worked as a waitress to supplement my self booked tours.

Through music and travel I learned the communication skills and dedication that support me now. Design work is not so different from waitressing. I am still eager to make sure the menu makes sense, careful in my communication with the cooks in back, and aiming for an outcome that is as expected or better.
